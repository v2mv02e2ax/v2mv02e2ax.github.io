Profile

 An assistant professor at Tohoku University. Fumiya Hori was born in 1998 in Taketa, Oita Prefecture, Japan. He became interested in science from a young age because of Galileo, a Japanese television drama. In 2025, he earned his Ph.D. from Kyoto University and is currently an assistant professor at Tohoku University.

Profile

　東北大学で物性物理学の実験的研究を行っています。大学院生時代は核磁気共鳴法(NMR)、核四重極共鳴法(NQR)を用いて、電子相関の強い希土類化合物の電子状態を原子レベルのミクロな視点から調べていました。
