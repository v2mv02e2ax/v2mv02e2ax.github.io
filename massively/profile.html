Profile

 A doctoral student in Kyoto University. In 1998, Fumiya Hori was born in Taketa, Oita Prefecture, Japan. He became interested in science from a yonung age because of "Galileo", Japanese television drama. He studied physics at Hiroshima University and graduated in 2020. He is now a first-year PhD student in Department of Physics, Kyoto University.

Profile

　京都大学で固体物理学の実験的研究を行っています。 最近は主に、核磁気共鳴法(NMR)、核四重極共鳴法(NQR)を用いて、 電子相関の強い希土類化合物の電子状態を原子レベルのミクロな視点から調べています。
