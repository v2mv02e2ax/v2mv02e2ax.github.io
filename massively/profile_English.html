He became interested in science from a yonung age because of "Galileo", Japanese television drama. He studied physics at Hiroshima University and graduated in 2020. Profile

　I specialize in low-temperature physics in Kyoto University. My research focuses on the experimental investigation of exotic materials manifesting novel quantum phenomena. Recently, I have mainly performed nuclear magnetic resonance (NMR)/nuclear quadrupole resonance (NQR) mesurements on rare-earth-based compounds with strong electron correlations to investigate the electronic states from a microscopic view.
